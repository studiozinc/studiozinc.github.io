Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026  ·  App: Urine Tracker (com.studiozinc.urinetracker)  ·  Contact: hello@studiozinc.com

🔒 Short version: Urine Tracker does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you log stays on your device and is only accessible to you.

1. Who We Are

Urine Tracker is developed and published by Studio Zinc, a subsidiary of ClickCircuit LLC. You can reach us at hello@studiozinc.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect no personal data.

Urine Tracker does not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information. The app functions entirely without an internet connection and without any form of registration.

The only data stored by the app is the drink log entries you create yourself (drink type, volume, and timestamp). This data is stored exclusively in local device storage using Android's standard SharedPreferences or Room database meaning it never leaves your device.

3. Data Storage

  • All app data is stored locally on your device.
  • No data is uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third-party platform.
  • No backups are made to external systems (unless you use your device's own Android Backup service, which is outside our control).
  • Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all associated data.

4. Internet Access

Urine Tracker does not require internet access to function. The app does not make any network requests, does not connect to any server, and does not transmit any data over the internet.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking libraries.

This includes, but is not limited to: Google Analytics, Firebase, Facebook SDK, Crashlytics, Adjust, AppsFlyer, or any other analytics or attribution platform.

6. Advertising

Urine Tracker contains no advertisements. We do not use any ad networks or display any third-party ads.

7. Permissions

The app may request the following Android system permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Only requested if you choose to enable optional drink reminders. Used solely to deliver local notification reminders you configure. No notification data is shared externally.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — May be used to restore your scheduled reminders if you restart your device. No personal data is involved.

No other permissions are requested. We do not access your contacts, camera, location, microphone, files, or any other sensitive device resources.

8. Children's Privacy

Urine Tracker is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since the app collects no personal data from any user at any age, it presents no specific risk to children's privacy.

9. Data Retention

We do not retain any data on our end, because we never receive any data in the first place. Your log data persists on your device until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app.

10. Your Rights

Since we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. If you wish to erase all app data, simply uninstall Urine Tracker from your device.

11. Medical Disclaimer

Urine Tracker is a personal logging utility. It is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or health condition. The app does not provide medical advice. Any information you log is for your own personal reference only.

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for guidance regarding your health, medical conditions, or fluid intake requirements.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

We will notify users of material changes through an in-app notice or via the Google Play Store listing where technically feasible.

13. Contact Us
